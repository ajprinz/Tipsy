Bloody Mary

Ingredients

  • 2 oz Premium Vodka
  • 2 oz Tomato Juice
  • 1 Lemon and Lime
  • Add to Taste: Salt, Pepper, Tobasco, Horseradish, and Paprika

Directions

  1. Squeeze lemon and lime wedges into a shaker and drop inside
  2. Add remaining ingredients and fill with ice
  3. Shake gently and strain into glass
