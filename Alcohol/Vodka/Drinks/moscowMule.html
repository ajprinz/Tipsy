Moscow Mule

Ingredients

  • 2 oz Premium Vodka
  • 0.5 oz Lime Juice
  • Splash Ginger Beer

Directions

  1. Combine the vodka and lime juice into a shaker
  2. Shake gently and pour into a short glass or Moscow mule mug
  3. Top with ginger beer
