Appletini

Ingredients

  • 1.5 oz Premium Vodka
  • 1 oz Cointreau
  • 0.5 oz Apple Pucker
  • 0.5 oz Simple Syrup

Directions

  1. Combine all of the ingredients into a shaker with ice
  2. Shake well and strain into martini glass
