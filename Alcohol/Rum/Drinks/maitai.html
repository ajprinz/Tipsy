Mai Tai

Ingredients

  • 1 oz Dark Rum
  • 1 oz Light Rum
  • 0.5 oz Orange Juice
  • 0.5 oz Pineapple juice
  • Splash Grenadine

Directions

  1. Combine light rum, orange juice and pineapple juice in a tall glass with ice
  2. Tilt cup and slowly add grenadine until it sinks to the bottom
  3. Float dark rum on top
