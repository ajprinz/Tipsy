Painkiller

Ingredients

  • 2 oz Dark Rum
  • 1 oz Pineapple Juice
  • 1 oz Orange Juice
  • 1 oz Cream of Coconut

Directions

  1. Add all of the ingredients into a shaker and fill with ice
  2. Shake well and strain into a tall glass filled with ice
